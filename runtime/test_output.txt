Backpack Runtime -- WebGPU inference from GGUF/ONNX models

Usage: .\backpack_runtime.exe --model <model.gguf or model-dir/>
  [--prompt <text>] [--max-tokens <n>] [--backend vulkan|d3d12]
  [--profile] [--benchmark] [--bench-detail]
  [--bench-prompt-len <n>] [--bench-gen-tokens <n>]

Supports:
  - GGUF files (Q8_0 quantization)
  - ONNX directories (model.onnx + config.json)
